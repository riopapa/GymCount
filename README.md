# Gym Count
**Gym Count** is the android application to assist self gym counting

- You can set various gym style (currently 6 cases) 
- Following are settable
  - Speed
  - Counts
  - Step count
  - Keep Staying after main count
  - Counting Up or Down
  - Say Ready or not, Say Start or not
    
<H1>Screenshots and How to operate</H1>

<H3> Main Screen</H3>

- There are several default cards for your various gym style, e.g. Jumping Jack, Squat, Prank, ..

- You may set card name at the top just for reference later

- <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/i_delete.png" width=24 height=24> on the right of **EXERCISE NAME** is for deleting that exercise type

- You may settle **SPEED**  <img src="./app/screenshots/speedKorean.png" width=24 height=24> , **MAIN COUNT**  <img src="./app/screenshots/countKorean.png"  width=24 height=24> by clicking number itself

- <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_step_on.png" width=24 height=24>&nbsp;&nbsp; will play **STEP COUNT** for each main count

- <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_hold_on.png" width=24 height=24>&nbsp;&nbsp;is for **HOLDING** some seconds after your excercise, e.g. 20 squat and the hold 10 secs with folding your knees

- <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_count_up.png" width=24 height=24>, <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_count_down.png" width=24 height=24> , <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_count_up5.png" width=24 height=24> , <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_count_down5.png" width=24 height=24>  is switch for counting UP or DOWN

- <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_count_up.png" width=24 height=24>, <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_count_down.png" width=24 height=24> says count ALL the count, but <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_count_up5.png" width=24 height=24> , <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_count_down5.png" width=24 height=24> says nothing while remaining count is FIVE. It will be weired if step flag is on. 

- <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_ready_on.png" width=24 height=24>&nbsp;&nbsp;is whether to say **READY** or not
- <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_start_on.png" width=24 height=24>&nbsp;&nbsp;is whether to say **START** or not
- By pressing <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/i_go_shout.png" width=24 height=24>&nbsp;&nbsp; Count up/down is started and <img src="./app/src/main/res/drawable/running_gifmaker.gif" width=24 height=24> (gif file) will run with you
- By pressing <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/i_stop.png" width=24 height=24>&nbsp;&nbsp; again counting will be stopped
- <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/i_stop.png" width=24 height=24>&nbsp;&nbsp; on top right button also can be used to stop shouting

- <img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_two.png" width=24 height=24>,<img src="./app/src/main/res/mipmap-xxhdpi/icon_three.png" width=24 height=24>&nbsp;&nbsp; on top is for switching column count.
